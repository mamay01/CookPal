Back Pork Menudo Instructions: 1. In a pan or wok, heat cooking oil

and atsuete oil. 2. Saute garlic, onion. Then add the pork,

liver, chorizo de Bilabo, tomatoes, bell pepper,

paprika, patis and the stock. 3. Cover and bring to a boil. Simmer for

20 minutes or until the pork is tender. 4. Add the chickpeas, potatoes and raisins.

Boil for another 2 minutes. 5. Salt and pepper to taste 6. Serve hot with white rice.
