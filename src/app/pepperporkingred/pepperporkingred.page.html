Back Pepper Pork Belly Instructions: 1. Heat cooking oil in a cooking pot.

3 tablespoons cooking oil. 2. Sauté onion and garlic. 1 onion,

4 cloves garlic. 3. Add pork belly once the onion softens.

Continue sautéing until the exterior of

the pork turns light brown. 2 lbs. pork belly. 4. Add Knorr Liquid Seasoning, oyster sauce,

soy sauce, and water. Let it boil.

Cover the pot. Adjust the heat to a low setting.

Cook until the pork becomes tender.

Note: add water as needed.

1 tablespoon Knorr Liquid Seasoning,

1 tablespoon oyster sauce,

3 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 cups water. 5. Add the sugar and put all the bell peppers

into the pot. Cook for 3 minutes.

1 red bell pepper,

1 orange bell pepper,

1 teaspoons brown sugar,

1 green bell pepper. 6. Combine cornstarch with 3 tablespoons

of water. Stir. Pour the mixture into the pot.

1 ½ teaspoons cornstarch. 7. Once the sauce thickens, season your dish

with salt and ground black pepper.

Salt and ground black pepper to taste. 8. Transfer to a serving plate.

Serve with rice. Share and enjoy!.
