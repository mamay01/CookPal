Back Pinoy Chicken Curry Ingredients: 2 lbs. chicken cut into serving pieces 1 tablespoon curry powder 1 piece potato cubed 4 cloves minced garlic 2 stalks celery sliced 1 piece red bell pepper sliced 2 pieces long green pepper 1 piece onion chopped 2 thumbs ginger cut into strips 2 cups coconut milk 1/2 cup all-purpose cream optional 1 cup water fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste
