Back Pancit Canton Instructions: 1. Boil water and then add salt.

Put the pancit canton noodles. Cook for 2 minutes.

Remove the noodles and set aside. 2. Using the remaining water, blanch the

vegetable ingredients for 1 to 2 minutes.

Remove from the pot and soak in ice cold

water for 30 seconds. Drain the water

and set the vegetables aside. 3. Heat oil in a clean cooking pot.

Saute onion and garlic until the onion

softens. 4. Add chicken breast, squid balls,

and Chinese sausage. Saute for 1 to

2 minutes. 5. Add the shrimp. Cook for 1 minute. 6. Pour the soy sauce in the cooking pot

and then add oyster sauce, and 1 cup water.

Let the liquid boil. 7. Add Knorr shrimp cube. Stir and gently toss-in

the pancit canton noodles and add the chopped

parsley. Cover the pot. Adjust the heat setting of

your stove between low and medium. Cook the

noodles for 2 to 3 minutes or until well done,

but not soggy (Note: add more water if needed). 8. Toss the blanched vegetables in the pot.

Season with salt and ground black pepper. 9. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with lemon

wedges or calamansi. Share and Enjoy!
