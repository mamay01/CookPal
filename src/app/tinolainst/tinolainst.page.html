Back             Chicken Tinola             Instructions:             1.  In a stock pot, heat oil and sauté garlic,

onion and ginger.                       2. Add water and the chicken                           3. Bring to a boil and simmer for about

20 minutes or until the chicken is almost done.                     4. Season with patis               5. Add papaya and continue to simmer for an

additional 5 minutes or until papaya

softens but not overcooked.               6. Add sili leaves then turn off the heat.                     7. Serve steaming hot on a bowl

with plain rice on the side.
